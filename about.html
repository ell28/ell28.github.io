About Lakers Nation


Lakers Nation is your home for Los Angeles Lakers coverage, news, analysis, rumors, and score updates. Working out of our offices in Southern California, we cover the team you love 24/7 in ways no mainstream media outlet can — from practices to game day and everything in between

We are first and foremost true die-hards and are proud of being Lakers fans. We are committed to keeping you up to date each day with all the latest from Lakerland. At the end of the day, we strive to be all things Lakers.
