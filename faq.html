How Many NBA Titles have the Lakers won?

1949, 1950, 1952, 1953, 1954, 1972, 1980, 1982, 1985, 1987, 1988, 2000, 2001, 2002, 2009, 2010, and 2020

What are the Lakers known for?

The Lakers are one of the most successful professional franchises in all American sports. The franchise has won a combined 17 NBA Titles.

Magic Johnson (3), Kareem Abdul-Jabbar (3), Shaquille O'Neal (1), Kobe Bryant (1)
